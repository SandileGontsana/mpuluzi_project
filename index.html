Mpuluzi DTDC e-Learning hub

The centre for ongoing professional development of teachers has just introduced an e-learning hub for teachers in Albert Luthuli sub district.

The centre comprises of 5 circuits i.e Mpuluzi Dundonald, Mashishila, Badplaas, and Carolina. Its mandate is teacher professionalisation.

Introducing the e-Learning hub

The Mpuluzi DTDC e-Learning Hub stands as a beacon of innovation and transformation in teacher professionalization within the Albert Luthuli sub-district. With a strong focus on integrating technology into education, the center is dedicated to equipping educators with the skills and tools needed to navigate the demands of a modern curriculum.

How? By fostering collaboration, driving innovation, and investing in professional development, the e-Learning Hub is shaping the future of teaching and learning, ensuring that teachers and students alike are prepared for the challenges of the 21st century.

Mission Statement

To empower teachers in the Albert Luthuli sub-district through professional development, focusing on integrating digital and e-learning competencies to enhance teaching practices and align with modern curriculum mandates.

Vision Statement

To be a leading e-learning hub driving teacher professionalization and fostering technological innovation in education, ensuring quality teaching and learning for all.

"Technology will never replace great teachers, but technology in the hands of great teachers can be transformational."

— George Couros

Core Values

Innovation: Adopting and promoting new technologies like MOODLE LMS for effective teaching.

Empowerment: Equipping teachers with TPACK (Technological, Pedagogical, and Content Knowledge) and 13 digital competencies.

Collaboration: Partnering with organizations like MICT SETA and affiliated schools.

Excellence: Supporting teacher development and enhancing assessment capabilities, such as e-assessment for Grade 12 learners.

Social Responsibility

Providing internships for IT students (Software Development, Business Analysis, etc.), fostering real-world skills and career development.

Supporting local schools in adapting to e-learning and digital transformation.

Facilitating the dissemination of educational resources and trainingfor teachers across multiple circuits, improving education accessibility and effectiveness.
